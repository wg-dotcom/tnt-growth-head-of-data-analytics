A note on selection

Your AMs are fixing dashboards every morning. That's a missing person problem.

Funnel.io disconnects. Supermetrics pulls stale data. A client asks for last week's numbers and someone on your team loses two hours chasing it down. It looks like a tooling problem. It's not. It's a missing infrastructure layer, and one person can fix it.

You need someone who can build API extractors directly into BigQuery, own the pipeline end to end, and get your Account Managers out of the reporting weeds so they can do actual client work. You also have a junior analytics team sitting at 0.5 that needs to get to 1. That means a technical lead who can set standards, review work, and develop the people who report to them.

We looked for that exact combination: people who have automated manual reporting that was costing hours, built connectors that don't break when the platform updates, and led junior analysts toward something more capable. Not just data people who know SQL. People who have been the upgrade in an analytics org before.

Every candidate in this shortlist went through a full process: application review, screening interview, and a hands-on technical assessment designed around TNT Growth's specific setup. What you're looking at is what made it through all of that. Your match is in here.
